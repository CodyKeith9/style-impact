Services

Tailored Styling Services

At Style Impact, we offer personalized styling services designed to fit your lifestyle— whether in person or virtual. Every service is customized to help you build a wardrobe that reflects who you are and supports how you live.

Personal Styling

A one-on-one session where Brian or a Style Impact expert curates outfits based on your body type, preferences, and lifestyle needs. We assess your wardrobe, recommend additions, and build versatile looks for work, events, and everyday life.

Expect mood boards, fitting guidance, and practical styling techniques to help you feel confident and put-together.

In-Person or Virtual

Closet Audit

A comprehensive review of your existing wardrobe to identify what’s working—and what’s not. This service includes organization, identifying key pieces, and recommending thoughtful edits or upgrades.

You’ll leave with a streamlined closet, outfit ideas, and a targeted shopping list that saves time, money, and guesswork.

Wardrobe Optimization

Virtual Styling

Perfect for clients on the go or at a distance. Through video consultations and digital tools, we provide personalized styling advice remotely.

Share photos of your wardrobe or inspiration and receive custom style guides, virtual try-on suggestions, and curated recommendations.

Remote Consultations

Personal Shopping

Let us handle the search. Based on your budget, preferences, and specific needs, we source and select pieces from trusted brands and retailers.

Whether for a special event or a seasonal refresh, you’ll receive hand-picked options with styling notes that make shopping seamless and enjoyable.

Curated Selection

Production Styling

Professional wardrobe styling for photo shoots, video productions, television, advertising campaigns, fashion shows, lookbooks, and social media content.

We collaborate closely with photographers, directors, and creative teams to translate concepts into polished, on-brand visuals with high-impact results.
